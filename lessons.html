Esports has become a major field of academic study

Ten colleges and universities offer degree programs in the esports field, including big name campuses like UC Irvine, the University of Texas, and The Ohio State University. Still more universities offer esports extracurricular opportunities and competitive teams. These degree programs cover topics ranging from the business aspects of managing esports teams to strategic statistical analysis and more.

Esports is so popular, Comcast is constructing a $50 million arena so fans can watch live!

Fusion Arena, first of its kind in the US, promises to be a game-changer in esports. Based in Philadelphia, the Fusion Arena will have space for 3,500 spectators of competitive gaming and other events. Construction is scheduled to complete by the end of this year.
